Rice

Jasmine rice is a type of fragrant long-grain rice that is primarily grown in Southeast Asia. It has a sweet and nutty flavor with a delicate, light, and fluffy texture.

ingredient

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 to 1 1/4 cups water (depending on method)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice.
  2. Combine the rice, water, and salt in a saucepan
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, cover, and simmer until the water is absorbed.
  4. Let the rice stand, then fluff before serving.
